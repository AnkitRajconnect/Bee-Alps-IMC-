Our Strategic Work

What we built for BeeAlp

A full integrated marketing communications strategy — from market intelligence through to a phased crowdfunding roadmap and social content system.

01 · RESEARCH
📊
Market & Competitive Analysis
Mapped 6 competitors (Apple, Samsung, Fairphone, Back Market, Commown, Grover) and identified a white space: no player integrates flagship performance + circular model + European sovereignty.
02 · POSITIONING
🎯
Targeting & Value Proposition
Phased targeting across 3 cohorts: Gen Z/Millennials (18–38) in years 1–3, Gen X (39–55) in years 4–6, then late majority switchers. Core promise: "The Never-Ending Premium Smartphone."
03 · CONTENT
📱
Social Media & Content Strategy
Built a full AIDA-funnel content system across LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Discord, and newsletters — with 5 content pillars, tone-of-voice guidelines, and a €45K social media budget plan.
04 · CROWDFUNDING
🚀
Crowdfunding Campaign Strategy
Designed the full September 2026 crowdfunding campaign: 30,000 warm contact waitlist, Day 1 target of 40% funding (€200K), pop-up demos in 3 cities, and influencer micro-network activation.
05 · COMMUNITY
🤝
UGC & Referral System
Designed an always-on referral program with "Continuity Benefits" (priority support, early OS access) instead of cash discounts — to protect premium brand perception while driving organic growth.
06 · B2B
🏢
B2B Corporate Channel
Identified that ~28% of EU companies provide mobile devices to employees. Developed corporate kit strategy targeting HR/IT directors via MEDDIC sales qualification — for bulk deployments and lower CAC.

Content Strategy

5 Content Pillars That Drive the Narrative

Every piece of content maps to one of these pillars — creating a coherent brand story without ever sounding preachy about sustainability.

⚡
Premium Continuity
Performance over time. "Year 3 and still fast" — proving the device doesn't slow down, backed by 8-year software support data.
👤
Phone as Mine
OS personalisation + MyBee Profile UGC. Shifting perception from "phone as a service" to "this phone is mine" — building emotional attachment.
🛡️
Care Included
Repair and replacement clarity. When something goes wrong, BeeAlp handles it — removing downside risk and surprise costs consumers dread.
💶
Smart Economics
Predictable, declining cost vs. the €900+ upgrade cycle. Subscription isn't debt — it's total cost of ownership that shrinks every year you stay.
♻️
Circular Proof
Lifecycle transparency without greenwashing. Factual, non-preachy sustainability — showing the circular loop with real data, not empty claims.

Growth Mechanics

UGC & Community-Led Growth

Designed two viral growth systems that work together — one for community identity, one for word-of-mouth acquisition.

🐝 MyBee Profile — UGC Engine
A saveable OS personalisation layer that transforms the phone from a commodity into a personal identity. Users share their setups, creating organic UGC and community content — while solving the psychological ownership barrier of subscription models.
